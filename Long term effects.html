Long term effects.

,

The United Nations University estimates that a jaw dropping 45 million tons of computers, televisions, mobile phones and other electronic goods are thrown away every year. Most of this e-waste is probably reusable and or repairable. Strikingly e-waste is on its way to reach 120 million tons per year.
