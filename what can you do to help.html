What you can do to help.

Consumption occurs everyday, for everyone multiple times a day. A very simple way of making a change, is by simply reflecting on how much you waste as in trash and even how much you consume, eating wise. Along with this a less frequent change you can make is when you damage or break something reflect on if it is repairable. If you see that you don't have your own solution, consider using a helpful website such as iFixit. iFixit is a an all free website with step by step instructions on how to fix damaged products.
